Smart Irrigation Tips

  • Water early morning or late evening to reduce evaporation.
  • Use mulching around plants to keep soil moisture longer.
  • For drip systems, check filters weekly to keep flow uniform.
  • Before irrigating, dig 5–7 cm to feel soil moisture by hand.
