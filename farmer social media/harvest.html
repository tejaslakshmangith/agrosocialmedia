Quick checklist to understand if your crop is ready for harvest.

Quality Grade Idea

Use this simple grading idea before selling or storing your produce.

  • Grade A: Healthy, uniform size, no visible pest marks.
  • Grade B: Minor spots but safe; sell quickly.
  • Grade C: Damaged or diseased; separate and avoid mixing.
